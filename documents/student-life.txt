The ideal college experience looks different for every student. At George Mason University, we believe that the experience you have beyond the classroom is just as integral as academic programs to nurturing your growth at every level. We actively foster an environment that ensures you find your place and your community here with other Patriots.

George Mason University is Virginia’s largest and most diverse public research university, with a commitment to meeting the needs of all our students. No matter your path to higher education, at George Mason you’ll find a place and a community that encourages your personal and professional growth.
