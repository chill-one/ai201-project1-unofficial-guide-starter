George Mason will help graduate students without teaching or research responsibilities to find grants, fellowships and awards.

These funding opportunities support:

Research Fieldwork Writing International experience Professional development Language study Post-doctoral training The Office of Fellowships

The Office of Fellowships works to recruit, mentor and support graduate students seeking external funding though nationally competitive awards. That support includes:

Information sessions, announcements, and search tools to identify funding opportunities. Fellowship advising and grant writing support. Workshops on how to craft strong applications. One-on-one coaching and feedback during the applicationprocess. Support for faculty mentors and advisors. Class visits, workshops, webinars, and lists of opportunities tailored by discipline. Contact the Office of Fellowships to set up an advising appointment or request information.

Qualifying Award Application: Per Policy 6001/6003 for Graduate Students with External Funding (Grants, Fellowships, or Awards) *Note: Applications must be submitted prior to July 1 prior to the Fall semester of which your external funding will go into affect. For questions, please email geawards@gmu.edu and fllwshps@gmu.edu.
